Why this view exists — decisions as first-class trace objects

Every other tab answers what happened. This one answers why the agent selected this action from the alternatives available to it — the difference between execution tracing and decision tracing. Each record below promotes a consequential choice (refund eligibility, high-risk tool use, escalation, guardrail override) into an inspectable object correlated to its evidence, policy checks, authority and business outcome by workflow_id. Rationale and confidence shown here are illustrative synthetic values — in production they must be emitted by the agent, never back-filled (see docs/decision-contract.md §5).
